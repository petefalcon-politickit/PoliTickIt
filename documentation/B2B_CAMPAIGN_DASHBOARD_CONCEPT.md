# B2B Concept: Campaign & Institution Dashboard

This document outlines the strategic vision for the "Publisher" side of the Participation Economy, allowing campaigns, PACs, and non-profits to visualize and engage with "Constituent Capital."

## 1. Executive Summary

While the mobile app focuses on constituent intelligence, the B2B dashboard provides the "Signal Output" view. It allows designated users (Representatives, Candidates) to see the real-time ripple effect of their policies and legislative actions.

## 2. Key Metrics for Campaigns

### Constituent Capital Index (CCI)

- **Definition**: The aggregate "Participation Credit" balance of verified constituents in a specific district.
- **Value**: Indicates the "Civic Intensity" of the district. A high CCI means a more engaged, alert, and potentially volatile electorate.

### Sentiment Ripple Velocity

- **Definition**: The speed at which users respond to a specific `PoliSnap` (Pulse) within the first 6 hours of publication.
- **Value**: Identifies "Flashpoint Issues" before they reach mainstream local media.

### Watchlist Saturation

- **Definition**: The percentage of constituents who have added a specific Bill or Rep to their persistent Watchlist.
- **Value**: Predicts long-term voter memory on specific issues.

## 3. The "Institution" View (Data Elements)

### A. District Heatmaps

- Visualizing hotspots of "Concern" vs "Support" geographically using verified constituent ZIP codes.

### B. Segmented Advocacy

- Identifying "Tier 4 (Predictive)" users who act as "Super-Constituents" due to their high engagement levels.

### C. Direct Response Pipeline

- Campaigns can "Sponsor" a Knowledge Snap (marked as such) to provide their official narrative directly into the feed of concerned users, earning "Transparency Points."

## 4. Monetization Strategy (B2B)

- **Subscription Tiers**: Access to real-time sentiment data vs. delayed/aggregated data.
- **Verification Fees**: Political entities can pay to have their profiles "Verified & Anchored" (High-fidelity headers).
- **Narrative Placement**: Managed via the "Distributor Processor" to ensure the official stance is automatically linked to relevant legislative snaps.

## 5. Ethical Failsafes

- **Anonymity**: No individual user PII is ever shared with campaigns. Data is strictly aggregated at the district/precinct level.
- **Non-Intervention**: Campaigns cannot manipulate the "Participation Credits" of users; capital is earned through interaction with the independent `PoliSnap` library.
