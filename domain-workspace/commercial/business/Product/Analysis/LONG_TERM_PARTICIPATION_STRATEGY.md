## 🏛️ 1. Progression Analysis (User Velocity)

We estimate the "Time to Intelligence" based on current reward weights (Pulse: 25, Watchlist: 50, Share: 75, Action: 100).

| Milestone           | Tier   | Credits | Est. Engagement                              | Est. Time |
| :------------------ | :----- | :------ | :------------------------------------------- | :-------- |
| **The Hook**        | Tier 1 | 0       | Initial App Install / Exploration.           | Day 0     |
| **The Analyst**     | Tier 2 | 500     | 10 Pulses + 5 Watchlist Adds.                | Day 3–5   |
| **The Auditor**     | Tier 3 | 1,200   | 20 Pulses + 10 Adds + 2 Shares.              | Week 2–3  |
| **The Institution** | Tier 4 | 2,500   | 40 Pulses + 20 Adds + 10 Shares + 2 Actions. | Month 2–3 |

_Observation: The jump from Tier 3 to Tier 4 is designed to be the "Engagement Chasm," where users must transition from passive reading to active "Direct Action" or consistent forensic analysis._

## ⚡ 2. Short-Term Engagement Levers

To sustain momentum during the climb to 2,500 CR:

### **The Trust Thread™ Streak**

- **Feature**: Bonus credits for 3 consecutive days of drilling down to the **PoliProof™** layer.
- **Engagement**: Encourages forensic depth rather than surface browsing.

### **District Signal Ripples**

- **Feature**: Social notifications when a user’s "Pulse" contributes to a significant change in a District’s Consensus Map.
- **Engagement**: Provides immediate feedback on the value of their individual signal.

### **Topic Mastery (Badges as Utility)**

- **Feature**: Earn "Fiscal Watchdog" or "Liberty Guardian" status by interacting with specific policy tags.
- **Utility**: Higher-tier badges could provide unique themes or monospaced font variants for the user's dashboard.

## 🚀 3. Long-Term Engagement (Post-Tier 4: 2,500+ CR)

The core challenge: **What happens when the map is fully unlocked?**

### **The Sovereignty Council (5,000+ CR)**

- **Privilege**: Users gain the ability to "Audit the Auditor." They can vote on the accuracy of AI-generated summaries and Predictive Odds.
- **Incentive**: Direct influence over the **Trust Thread™** accuracy and signal integrity.

### **Consensus Weighting (The Seniority Signal)**

- **Feature**: High-participation pulses carry a "Significance Multiplier" in the global Consensus Ripple.
- **Incentive**: Their civic voice literally becomes more powerful as they prove their commitment to the chain of trust.

### **Verified Citizen Analyst Credentials**

- **Feature**: Ability to generate **PoliProof™ Certified Reports** (PDF/JSON) for submission to local representatives or community boards.
- **Incentive**: Translates digital participation into professional-grade civic advocacy.

## 🤝 4. 501 Partnership Cross-Reference (The B2B Synergy)

Our 501(c)(3) partners are the primary drivers of "High-Value Credits."

### **The "Direct Action" Bridge**

- **Action**: A partner organization (e.g., "Citizens for Accountability") posts a town hall event.
- **Verification**: User attends and triggers an **Attendance PoliProof™** (Geo-fence/QR).
- **Reward**: Massive credit injection (+250) to accelerate tier progression.

### **Partner-Endorsed Intelligence**

- **Feature**: 501s can "curate" specific legislative drills. Users who follow a partner's curated **Trust Thread™** earn "Focus Credits."
- **Win-Win**: Partners get higher engagement on their specific issues; users climb tiers faster through high-quality data paths.

## 🎯 5. Strategic Recommendations

1.  **Introduce T5 (Governance)**: At 5,000 CR, move from "consuming" intelligence to "validating" it.
2.  **Visual Progression**: The UI should gradually become more "Forensic" (more monospaced elements, more data density) as the user climbs tiers, reflecting their increasing sophistication.
3.  **Gamified Scarcity**: Release "Flash Participation Events" coordinated with 501 partners that offer limited-time credits.
