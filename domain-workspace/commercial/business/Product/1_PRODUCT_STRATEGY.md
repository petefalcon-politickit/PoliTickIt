# PoliTickIt Product Strategy: Vision, Execution & Roadmap

**Mantra: Accountability. Trust. Verifiability. Community.**

This document serves as the high-fidelity source of truth for the **PoliTickIt Product Strategy**. It defines the MVP scope, feature manifest, gamification logic, and tactical roadmap.

---

## 🏛️ 1. MVP Product Scope

The goal for MVP is to deliver **Daily High-Frequency Content** at the Federal level combined with **Multi-Dimensional Audits** that treat "Collective" features as high-fidelity engagement rewards.

### **The Gold Standard for MVP**

- **Home Feed**: Must feel "Alive," with updates reflecting the last 24–48 hours of Congressional, Financial, and Ethical activity.
- **Feed Enrichment Pipeline**: Automated variety reordering to prevent representative clusters and ensure a dynamic "Institutional Track" experience.
- **Audit Collective Layer**: High-value collective insights (ROI Audit, Predictive Scoring) are **Earnt, not Bought**.
- **Mechanical UI Aesthetic**: Adopt a high-density, monospaced-accented design language to emphasize the "Institutional" and "Forensic" nature of the data.
- **Institutional Navigation**: High-density Drawer and Header systems optimized for professional forensic analysis, featuring Significance-based Intensity Gating™.
- **The Trust Thread™ (The Drill-Down Continuum)**: A verifiable 1-2-3 punch from High-Level Accountability to Raw Forensic Proof.
- **PoliProof™ (The Chain of Trust)**: The gold standard for verifiable civic data.

### **Core Content Domains**

- **Congressional Record**: Daily summaries, debate highlights, and rapid floor votes.
- **Legislation Docket**: Upcoming votes, bill steppers, and layman position summaries.
- **Finance Engine (FEC)**: Campaign finance snaps, influence correlations, and PAC/Corporate tracing.
- **Committee Intelligence**: Hearing schedules, member insights, and transcript highlights.
- **Rep Productivity**: Attendance records and achievement logs.

## 💎 2. The Participation Economy (Gamification)

**Mantra: "Intelligence Earned. Not Bought."**

### **The Sovereignty Ledger**

Access to advanced intelligence is earned through **verified civic engagement**. Credits are awarded for first-time interactions only:

- **Pulse Sentiment (+25)**: Nuanced reaction on a -10 to +10 scale.
- **Watchlist Add (+50)**: Bookmarking high-value insights.
- **Intelligence Share (+75)**: Redistributing insights.
- **Direct Action (+100)**: Attending town halls or calls-to-action.

### **Intelligence Tiers**

| Tier       | Name          | Requirement   | Core Benefit                                |
| :--------- | :------------ | :------------ | :------------------------------------------ |
| **Tier 1** | Standard      | 0 Credits     | Daily tracking and public sentiment.        |
| **Tier 2** | Intelligence  | 500 Credits   | **FEC Donor Correlation Maps**.             |
| **Tier 3** | ROI Auditor   | 1,200 Credits | **Full Accountability Scorecards**.         |
| **Tier 4** | Institutional | 2,500 Credits | **AI-Powered Analytics & Predictive Odds**. |

### **The Slug Strategy**

We replace "Paywalls" with **Discovery Slugs**:

- **Nudge Slugs**: Suggestions for earning credits.
- **Gated Feature Slugs**: High-density "teasers" explaining the value of the locked intelligence.

## 🏛️ 5. Institutional Monetization: The Representative Track

**Mantra: "True Sentiment. Verified Constituents."**

PoliTickIt creates a B2B marketplace by providing elected officials with high-fidelity, verified constituent sentiment data without compromising the **Local Sovereignty** of the user.

### **The "District View" (Consensus Ripple)**

- **Verified Residency**: Credits earned in the app act as a "Civic Reputation" score. Only users with a threshold of participation and verified district residency (managed locally via the Sovereignty Ledger) are included in the institutionally-sold sentiment stream.
- **Statistical Signal vs. Individual Identity**: We push anonymized, aggregated sentiment clusters (The Consensus Ripple) to our cloud telemetry. The representative sees a heat-map of district intent, not individual user profiles.
- **The Service Subscription**: Candidates and Incumbents pay for a **"Representative Dashboard"** to access real-time feedback loop analytics. This provides them with:
  - **Roll-Call Alignment**: How their actual district constituents feel vs. how the Rep is voting.
  - **Vulnerability Heatmaps**: Identifying policy areas where constituent sentiment is drifting away from the party line.
  - **Direct Response Utility**: The ability to issue "Responsive Snaps" that appear in the feeds of their specific constituents as "Formal Rebuttals" or clarifications.
