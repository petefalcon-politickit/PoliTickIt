# Trust Thread Image Prompts: Forensic Aesthetics

**Style Guide for PoliSnap Visual Assets**

## 1. Visual Direction: "The Archival Lens"

Every background image or visual hint in the PoliTickIt platform must signify authority and forensic history.

- **Primary Motif**: Archival paper, ink-stamped blueprints, monochromatic photography, architectural details of government buildings.
- **Lighting**: High-contrast, dramatic shadows, "The Light of Disclosure."

## 2. DALL-E 3 / Midjourney Prompts

### A. The Sovereignty Ledger

> "A close-up, high-detail photograph of an antique metal ledger book with a modern smartphone resting on its ivory-colored pages. The smartphone displays a monochromatic bar chart. Cinematic lighting, deep shadows, archival texture."

### B. The Consensus Ripple

> "A top-down architectural blueprint of a city district. A gentle water ripple effect is layered over the map in a soft, glowing cyan hue. Technical annotations, 19th-century drafting style, forensic aesthetic."

### C. The Institutional Core

> "A monolithic marble hallway leading to an open doorway with brilliant light pouring out. Archival film grain, monochromatic, high-authority, minimalist."

## 3. Deployment

These images are used for:

- Snap Backgrounds (for low-density narrative snaps).
- B2B Dashboard Hero sections.
- Marketing Pitch Decks ("Sovereignty as a Service").
