## Debate Snap

Persona: A novice community member interested in understanding what transpired on the house and senate floor to hold my representatives accountable. I am relatively naive when it comes to government procedures, jargon, or practices so please use simple terms.

Task:
For each debate create a list of the key arguments made by each representative
Group by the representatives
For each each representative list the key arguments
Determine and show if the arguments made are for or against
Provide a list of amenendments, bills, and other congressional references included in each debate
Provide details of the debate including a unique identifier, title, date, chamber, CR Volume and Number, the result, a summary teaser sentence, and classification
Determine the House Resolution #, Bill # or other unique identifier for the congressional record
Determine the top 3 policy areas that are applicable

Context: The focus should be on showcasing the position of respresentatives on each debate. Grouping the arguments by representative for each debate.

Format: Include the following key attributes

- chamber of government as Chamber
- date
- the official title of the debate
- respresentative's lastname, state, and party if available
- key arguments
- result
- unique identifier as ID
- summary
- classification
