# Trust Thread™: Image Generation Prompts

These prompts were ideated on January 28, 2026, to create a high-fidelity raster/SVG asset for the **Trust Thread™** call-to-action. The goal is to move from code-based UI to a specialized "Institutional Asset" that maintains design control and accents.

---

## **Design Identity: Institutional-Forensic**

**Primary Colors:** Navy (#164570), Slate (#F8FAFC), Rose (#E11D48)  
**Font Preference:** Monospaced, All-Caps, Technical.

---

## **Prompt 1: The Forensic Ribbon (Cyber-Intelligence)**

> "A high-resolution UI button asset for a political forensic app. The design is a long horizontal ribbon. Background is a deep bureaucratic navy blue (#164570) with a faint, technical 1px white coordinate grid and microscopic data telemetry numbers in the corners. In the center, the text 'TRUST THREAD™' is written in a crisp, sharp white monospaced font. The bottom edge has a glowing, thin emerald green pulse line. Style: Cyber-forensic, high-fidelity, minimalistic, industrial design."

## **Prompt 2: The Institutional Receipt (Official/Banknote)**

> "A professional UI graphic representing a 'Verifiable Civic Audit.' The design is a horizontal banner with a light slate-blue-to-white gradient background. The background features complex, microscopic 'security guilloche' patterns similar to those found on a U.S. banknote or a high-security passport. On the left, sharp navy blue text reads 'TRUST THREAD™' in a technical font. On the far right, a subtle circular watermark of a shield. Style: Official government document, transparent, clean, institutional."

## **Prompt 3: The Mechanical Block (Bauhaus/Brutalist)**

> "A brutalist-style UI button design for a data-heavy application. The design is a heavy, solid dark slate rectangle. The rectangle is split into two sections by a heavy vertical line with diagonal blue-and-gray warning stripes. On the left, bold white text says 'TRUST THREAD™.' Faintly in the background of the dark slate, the numbers '1-2-3' are embossed in a large, technical typeface. Style: Bauhaus, mechanical engineering, accountability, high-contrast, modern industrial."

---

## **Universal Stylistic Constraints**

- **Aspect Ratio:** 4:1 (Horizontal Ribbon)
- **Negative Prompts:** No shadows, no 3D buttons, no bevels, no rounded corners, no bubbly text, no cartoonish style.
- **Finish:** Flat UI, high-density informatics.
