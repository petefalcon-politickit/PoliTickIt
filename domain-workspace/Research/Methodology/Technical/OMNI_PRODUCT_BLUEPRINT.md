## 📄 Executive Summary

**Omni-OS** is a platform-agnostic, hierarchical AI governance kernel designed to solve the "Governance Debt" in high-velocity AI development. By decoupling the universal orchestration logic (**The Kernel**) from project-specific constraints (**The Manifest**), Omni-OS provides a deterministic, self-policing environment for non-deterministic AI agents.

---

## 🏛️ System Architecture: Kernel vs. Manifest

The core novelty of the Omni-OS product is its **Hierarchical Inheritance Model**.

### 1. The Omni-OS Kernel (Systemic Core)

The Kernel represents the permanent, systemic guardrails required for any forensically sound AI environment.

- **Recursive Scaffolding**: The meta-factory that manifestations domain managers.
- **Chain-of-Verification (CoVe)**: The synchronous auditor pass that prevents logic drift.
- **Forensic Continuity Protocol (FCP)**: The deterministic state-handshake between execution and history.
- **Constitutional DNA**: Mandatory metadata headers for tracing and accountability.

### 2. The Project Manifest (Adaptive Domain)

The Manifest is a hot-swappable configuration layer that injects domain-specific enforcements into the Kernel.

- **Moat Definition**: Project-specific security/sovereignty rules (e.g., PoliTickIt's Zero-PII).
- **Rhythm Standards**: Project-specific UI/Layout mechanical grids.
- **Industry Compliance**: Regulatory constraints (HIPAA, PCI, Civic Dividend).
