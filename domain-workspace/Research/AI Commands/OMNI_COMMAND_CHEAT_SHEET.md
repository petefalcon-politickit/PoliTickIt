# **OMNI-COMMAND Cheat Sheet**

The `OMNI-COMMAND` (Omnipotent Command) suite is the high-velocity operational layer of the **PoliTickIt** platform. It allows the Visionary to delegate complex domain management to specialized AI Orchestrators.

---

## 🛠️ Command Specifications

### 1. The Global Anatomy

Every Omni-Command consists of two discrete anchors:

- **The Prompt** (`Coding Prompts/[name].md`): The "Brain" that instructs the AI on outcomes and triggers.
- **The SOP** (`documentation/AI Commands/[name]-command.md`): The "Manual" that defines rules, inheritance, and task checklists.

### 2. Mandatory Inheritance

All commands MUST inherit from the [Global AI Command Guardrails](global-command-guardrails.md).

- **Trigger Prefix**: `OMNI-`
- **Safety**: "Ask Before Delete" is non-negotiable.
- **Forensic Standards**: 4px vertical rhythm (`v-rhythm-tight-1`) and high-contrast typography.

### 3. Parameters

- `ideation-mode: true`: Activates the "Refine-Before-Generate" protocol. Use this to brainstorm command logic before committing to the filesystem.
