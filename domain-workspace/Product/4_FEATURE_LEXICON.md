## 🏛️ The PoliSnap™ (The Atomic Truth)

A **PoliSnap** is the fundamental unit of information in PoliTickIt. Unlike a traditional news article, a Snap is a self-contained "packet of truth" designed for high-velocity absorption.

- **Forensic Precision**: Every Snap focuses on one specific event—a vote, a donation, or a statement.
- **Modular Architecture**: Snaps are built from "Molecules" (like the Consensus Ripple or Legislative Stepper) that can be reassembled to give you the exact view you need.
- **Significance-First**: We use **Intensity Gating™** to push high-impact Snaps to the top, ensuring you see what matters most to your district first.

## 🛡️ The Consensus Ripple™ (The District View)

The **Consensus Ripple** is a high-fidelity visualization designed to bridge the gap between individual noise and collective power. It translates millions of verified data points into a single, forensic snapshot of a district’s true pulse.

### 1. The Ripple Map: Visualizing Momentum

Instead of a static chart, we use **spatial mapping**.

- **The Rings**: The concentric circles represent the "reach" of a policy or sentiment. The wider the ripple, the more deeply an issue is felt across the district.
- **Cluster Nodes**: Floating identifiers (e.g., "Labor," "Tech," "Verified Residents") show exactly which communities are driving the current trend.
  - **Green Node**: High alignment with the representative.
  - **Blue/Primary Node**: Divergence or emerging shift.

### 2. Constituent Capital

We measure **Constituent Capital**—a proprietary metric calculating the "volume" of intent. It’s not just about a simple "Yes" or "No," but the intensity and duration of engagement. This turns public opinion into a tangible form of influence that institutional stakeholders cannot ignore.

### 3. ZK-Verified Trust (Forensic Proof)

Privacy is built into the visualization's DNA using **ZK (Zero-Knowledge) Verification**.

- Every node on the map is a **Verified Reporter**.
- The system proves they are actual residents of that specific district without ever accessing personal identities.
  It’s like having an official receipt for the "Truth"—the Consensus Ripple is a verified record of the district’s will, anchored to the **Sovereignty Ledger**.

### 4. The Trust Thread™: Forensic Provenance

Every high-tier visualization is secured by a **Trust Thread**. Just like a bank note has a security strip, every Consensus Ripple snap is signed and serialized (e.g., `REF: PS-XXXX`). This ensures data integrity and provides a "PoliProof™" receipt of accountability.

## 📊 ROI Report Card (Liquid Logic)

The **ROI Report Card** (Return on Interest) is the definitive diagnostic tool for constituent alignment.

### 1. The Alignment Score

A real-time correlation between your personal pulses (your "votes" in the app) and your representative's actual voting record. It answers the fundamental question: _Is my representative actually representing me?_

### 2. Intensity-Weighted Logic

Unlike traditional polling, our **Liquid Logic** engine weights your sentiment by intensity. If you pulse strongly (0.9+) on an issue, your representative's vote on that specific bill has a higher impact on your personal Alignment Score.

## 🌑 The Shadow Registry (Dynamic Resilience)

The **Shadow Registry** is our architectural failsafe for an evolving political landscape. As new types of data emerge (like new forensic metrics or community initiatives), the app doesn't need an update to show them.

- **Forensic Placeholders**: If the server sends a new data type the app hasn't seen before, the Shadow Registry renders a "Forensic Placeholder."
- **Data Integrity**: You still see the raw, verified data immediately, ensuring you're never "blind" to new information while the visual library catches up.
